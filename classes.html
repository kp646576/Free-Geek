About

Free Geek offers a range of educational opportunities including hands-on experiences in our Build and Adoption Programs as well as a wide variety of desk-based learning in our classroom. All classes are free to our volunteers and the general public. Please sign up in advance by visiting the volunteer desk at Free Geek or calling us at 503-232-9350.

Each year our Board of Directors ask for feedback during our Town Hall Meetings. Please view this response document to see what we’ve been working on in relationship to your 2014 feedback on our education programs.

We’re always looking for new volunteer teachers. No previous experience required! Gain teaching skills while giving back to your community. Find out more and apply online.

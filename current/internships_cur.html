<div class="entry"><h1>Internships</h1>
<p>An internship at Free Geek is an excellent opportunity to gain valuable job skills, make a difference for people and the environment, and make connections in the community. Each internship lasts between 3 to 6 months, with options to renew afterwards. Due to the nature of these volunteer activities, we ask that applicants be at least 16 years of age or older.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Active interns receive a 20% discount on items in the Free Geek Thrift Store and are eligible to become a member of the First Tech Credit Union. Free Geek also offers free computer classes to volunteers. In addition, interns can choose to receive a free computer after volunteering 24 hours in our internship program.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All internships at Free Geek are unpaid.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<hr size="2" />
<p>Below are links to the internships currently available at Free Geek. Click on any of them for a detailed description of the internship.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><b>In addition to the qualities mentioned in the internship descriptions below, all prospective interns should display the following qualities:</b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><b><b>Effective communication skills</b></b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><b>Punctuality and accountability</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><b>Patience and analytical thinking skills<br />
</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><b>Ability to self-motivate and work effectively without direct supervision</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><b>Ability to prioritize tasks and be flexible in a fast-paced work environment</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><b>Comfort working alongside volunteers from a wide variety of backgrounds</b></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h3>How to apply</h3>
<ol>
<li>Figure out which internship matches your interests. You&#8217;ll find short descriptions of each internship as well as links to longer descriptions above.</li>
<li><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/12pLjBHjYufqp_Z4bDCntMNnBPQRcb_JYkia5G0Gk1Lo/viewform">Fill out our intern application form</a>.</li>
<li>A Free Geek staff member will get back to you within two weeks to plan for an interview, if it seems like you may be a good fit for the position. We ask that almost all interns take a tour before interviewing.</li>
<li>Take a tour of Free Geek to check out our facility and culture. We offer tours at 11am and 4pm every day Tuesday through Saturday.</li>
<li>Come in for an interview and we&#8217;ll see if you&#8217;re a good fit for Free Geek, and you can decide if Free Geek is a good fit for you.</li>
</ol>

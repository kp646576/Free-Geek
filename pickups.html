Pickups

The Free Geek truck is ready for your pickup

Yes, Free Geek does pickups! Please contact us at least one week in advance for the best service. Our 16′ truck is generally available Wednesdays and Thursday, but may be available other days by prior arrangement. We charge a base rate of $120 for pickups, depending on quantity and composition of your material. We can invoice you and accept payment via cash, checks, online payments, and credit cards (in person).

Free Geek takes the security of your data very seriously. We make every effort to ensure any data-containing gizmos we receive are either wiped clean using Department of Defense standards, or physically destroyed on-site. As we do not currently offer certificates of destruction, the ultimate responsibility for the security of your data is yours. Here are some extra steps you can take to ensure your data is secure.

Find out more about what hardware we accept, as well as our participation in Oregon E-Cycles. And because we’re a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, you’re able to write off your hardware and financial donations as allowed by law.
